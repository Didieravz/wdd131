Emeralds: Nature's Green Treasures

Discover the allure of Colombian emeralds, known for their vibrant green color and exceptional quality.

Emerald Characteristics

  • Color: Deep, vivid green
  • Hardness: 7.5-8 on the Mohs scale
  • Clarity: Often contains inclusions, known as "jardin"
  • Origin: Primarily from the Muzo mines in Colombia

Buying Guide

When purchasing an emerald, consider the four Cs: Color, Clarity, Cut, and Carat weight. Our expert gemologists are available to guide you through the selection process.
